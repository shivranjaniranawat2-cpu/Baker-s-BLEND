About Us

Welcome to Baker’s Blend – where European elegance meets the warmth of home.

Nestled in the quiet charm of Madhuban, Udaipur, Baker’s Blend is more than just a café — it’s a thoughtfully curated escape for those who appreciate fine baking, comforting flavors, and beautiful spaces.

Founded by Gitanjali Rathore, our café was born from a vision to elevate Udaipur’s dessert culture with a touch of European artistry. From handcrafted custom cakes and artisanal cheesecakes to wood-fired pizzas, fluffy baos, and gourmet pastas, every creation is made with uncompromising quality and a deep passion for the craft.

Step into our pastel-hued sanctuary, where every detail is designed to make you feel at ease. Whether you're working over coffee, sharing laughter over board games, or enjoying a quiet moment with your furry companion, Baker’s Blend is a space where moments turn into memories.

We don’t just serve food — we craft experiences, where every slice, every sip, and every visit feels truly special. 🌿✨
